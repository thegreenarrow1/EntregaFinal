About me

Since I was a child, I had a great passion for drawing and building houses. Over the years, this hobby grew and my tastes for graphic design gradually increased until it was time to make the most important decision that a teenager can have, to choose what their educational project will be with the one who will grow as a professional and will be able to achieve support that allows him to live a life to the fullest and enjoy what he does.

From the beginning of my career to train as a graphic design professional, I had a great passion and attraction for the different projects that I was facing as a student. In 2017 I finally received my Bachelor's Degree in Design and started working on what I was so passionate about.

Over the years my ambition led me to discover the world of programming, which made me realize that it was something I wanted to complement my work as a graphic designer with. This is how I am currently training as a programmer.
